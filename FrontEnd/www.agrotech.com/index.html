ABOUT AGROTECH

Agriculture is a crucial sector that involves farming activities like growing crops and raising animals for various purposes. It is essential for food production, providing sustenance for people globally. Agriculture has been a fundamental part of human civilization, evolving over centuries with advancements in technology and practices. In countries like India, agriculture plays a significant role in the economy and livelihoods of many people. Agriculture is not just about farming; it encompasses a wide range of activities that contribute to food security, economic growth, and environmental sustainability
Agrotech is purely based on knowledge of crop protection and management. We aim to give and deliver comprehensive coverage of the latest developments in the field of agriculture, biotechnology, crop protection chemicals, with primary focus on product development, market information, regulatory guidelines of agrochemicals, company news, environmental news, country reports etc.
